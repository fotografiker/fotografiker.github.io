Technology Collective for Civil Society

We, as individuals specialized in the field of civil society, software development and creativity, have come together to form this collective with the aim of facilitating access to technology, increasing technology literacy and creating financial and temporal efficiency in the field of civil society.

While our most important priority is developing software tailored to the needs of civil society, we also aim to increase awareness and knowledge in the field of technology and ensure the efficient use of resources in this field.

Our fundamental principle is to develop and use technologies that reflect civil society values, respect people, are solution-oriented, accessible and do not introduce new cognitive loads.

In our fully flexible and inclusive operations, we value determining our needs together and producing solutions together.

What we do?


Software Development
Developing software related to the workflow, projects, finance, events, volunteer and employee management of Civil Society Organizations.
AI Applications in the Workplace
Facilitating daily workflow by using existing or custom-developed AI applications for natural language programming, object recognition, audio and visual classification, question answering, summarization, sentiment analysis, and translation.
Data Visualization and Mapping
Creating modern graphics and interactive tables from existing data sets, and performing mapping activities with GIS and similar systems.
Data Collection and Analysis
Gathering public data, including from social media, and applying AI algorithms on them for functions such as classification, sentiment analysis, and translation.
Mobile Games and Applications
Developing mobile games and applications specifically for Civil Society Organizations, including input methods such as sensors and cameras.
IoT Applications
Making existing devices smarter, remotely managing devices, developing smart office solutions.
Cybersecurity and AI Training for Adults
Increasing the awareness of an average user about current cyber risks and informing them about AI solutions that can facilitate their work life.
No-Code/Low-Code Software Training for Children
Providing no-code/low-code software training to children, taking into account age groups, in order to enable them to participate in technology production at an early age.
Technology Solution Partnership
Sometimes we may not need to provide you with a technology/software product directly, but we can prepare technical specifications for the software companies you are considering working with, or manage quality control and testing processes for completed works.
